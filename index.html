ABOUT ME

I am data focused professional passionate about helping organizations bring data solutions to life. I started my foray into tech as a Front End developer for an identity management organization. This nurtured my creativity and attention to detail, a skill which helped me create lovely data visualizations.

I pivoted to data science in 2020, by taking Udacity's Deep Learning Nanodegree, after which I went for my Masters' in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. During my Masters' studies I was privileged to focus on software, artificial intelligence and business courses.

I have spent most of the last year in data engineering and analytics capabilities at one of Canada's largest retailers. Performing analysis and building data pipelines for a number of business functions including fraud detection, equipment failure detection and price recommendation for products close to expiry has been a rewarding experience.
